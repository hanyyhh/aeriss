Our Story

From humble beginnings to a global movement, here's how AERIS evolved over the years.

01/04/2024

AERIS was founded with a vision to create sustainable solutions for a greener future. Our first product, an eco-friendly water bottle, was launched.

15/04/2024

We expanded our product line to include solar-powered gadgets and partnered with local communities to promote renewable energy.

14/11/2024

AERIS won the Green Innovation Award for our groundbreaking work in sustainable packaging.

22/01/2025

We launched our global initiative, "Green Earth," to plant 1 million trees worldwide and reduce carbon emissions.
